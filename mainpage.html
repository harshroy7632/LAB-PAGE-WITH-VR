About the Department

The Department of Aerospace Engineering at IIST offers comprehensive undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs in the field of aerospace engineering. Our programs provide a solid foundation in aerospace engineering principles, coupled with hands-on experience in various aerospace technologies.

Facilities

The Aerospace Department is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including wind tunnels, flight simulators, and materials testing laboratories. These facilities enable students and researchers to conduct cutting-edge experiments and simulations to advance the field of aerospace engineering.
